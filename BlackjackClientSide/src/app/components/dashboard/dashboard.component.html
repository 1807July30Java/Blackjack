About BlackJack Online

Blackjack (aka 21) is a card game involving a standard deck of playing cards. Each card has a value going from one to 11. The cards 2 through 10 are worth their respective numbers. Kings, queens, and jacks are each worth 10. Aces are a special case and may be used as either a 1 or an 11.

The player is given 2 cards to start, and through a process of drawing (hitting) the player must attempt to get the sum value of all their cards to as close to 21 wihtout going over.
